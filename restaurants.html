My Favourite Vegan Restaurants In Salt Lake City

Bud's

Type: Sandwiches

Best thing on the menu: Italian cold cut

Passion Flour Patisserie

Type: Café

Best thing on the menu: French dip sandwich or saint horn croissant

Eats Bakery and Coffee

Type: Café

Best thing on the menu: "Sausage, egg, and cheese" breakfast sandwich or any of the doughnuts

All Chay

Type: Vietnamese

Best thing on the menu: Ocean love rice or ginger tofu banh mi

Vertical Diner

Type: American

Best thing on the menu: Mac cheeze bites

MonkeyWrench

Type: Ice Cream

Best thing on the menu: The whole menu changes seasonally, but everything I've had has been excellent!

Veggie House

Type: Asian Fusion

Best thing on the menu: Lo mein with "beef"
